Danielle Bargovic

For as long as I can remember maps have always been a source of fascination and discovery in my world. The art of cartography has provided me with a true sense of North where my career is built and fueled by my deep love and appreciations for all things map related.

For the last 11 years my primary focus has been static cartographic map production using Geographic information Systems (GIS). I specialize in spatial data analysis, GPS data collection techniques and spatial data manuipulation and management.

Most recently my focus has turned to the art of web map design and the development of online web applications. This new path has furthered my overall appreciation of cartographic knowledge and shone a light on cartography's evolution and relevance in our modern day world.
