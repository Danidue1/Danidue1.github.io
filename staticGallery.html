#SustainabilityGoals

This static map graphic provides a visual for audiences, at public meetings, to view impoverished areas, within the Capital Region of New York, in relation to vacant lands.

This map was generated in ArcGIS using readily accessible American Community Survey data and parcel data classified as vacant.

Clinton's Ditch

A static map graphic displaying the recreational camping opportunities along the Erie Canal in New York State. The map also includes the lock numbers and canalway trails for geographic reference.

This map was generated in ArcGIS utilizing state recreation and transportation datasets freely available on the NYS GIS Clearinghouse.

Town of Salina Zoning Map

The official Town of Salina Zoning Map generated for display at the Salina Town Hall and within the town's comprehensive plan.

This map was generated in ArcGIS. The zoning data was created by replicating hard copy versions of the zoning map. Updates are made on an as needs basis with guidance from the town planning and development department.
