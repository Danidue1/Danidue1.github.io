Commuting at its finest

A basic point and line webmap representing the various stops one can make on their daily work commute. The points are representative of popular destinations such as a library, coffee house or gas station. While the purple "bread crumb trail" illustrates the path most heavily traveled.

The interactive map is written in JavaScript utlizzing the Leaflet API.

Lighting up the night sky

A webmap displaying graduated circles representing the energy production of select power plants in the U.S. The 2 colors represent natural gas produced energy in conjunction with coal (blue) and petroleum (green).

The interactive map is written in JavaScript utlizing the Leaflet API.

#SustainabilityGoals

This static map graphic provides a visual for audiences, at public meetings, to view impoverished areas within the region and in relation to vacant lands.

This map was generated in ArcGIS using readily accessible American Community Survey data and parcel data classified as vacant.

Clinton's Ditch

A static map graphic displaying the recreational camping opportunities along the Erie Canal in New York State. The map also includes the lock numbers and trails for geographic reference.

This map was generated in ArcGIS utilizing state recreation and transportation datasets freely available on the NYS GUS Clearinghouse.

Town of Salina Zoning Map

The official Town of Salina Zoning Map generated to be on display at Salina Town Hall and within the town's comprehensive plan.

This map was generated in ArcGIS. The zoning data was created by replicating hard copy versions of the zoning map. Updates are made on an as needs basis with guidance from the town planning and development department.
